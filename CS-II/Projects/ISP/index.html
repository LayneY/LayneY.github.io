Computer Science II - ISP


Summary

My ISP for this year is to help make the Model Scheduler app for Allen High School. Specifically, I will be working on the frontend, and using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to make the pages of the app. The app will be available for all incoming sophomores, juniors, and seniors to use to plan for Arena Scheduling. Arena Scheduling, the process that allows students to build their own schedules, can be difficult, especially for incoming sophomores. This app will make it easy for students to make multiple plans and backups for their schedules for the 2023-2024 school year. The app will be used by thousands of Allen High School students to plan their schedules for Arena.
